NOTIFICATION TO VOLUNTEERS.




A Volunteer can be a part of three teams but should be a core member of one particular Team.

Volunteer should Actively participate in Meetings, Events and other ocassions organised by Chennai Youngsters.

Volunteers should respond to the Notification, Announcments made by the Co-ordinators, Heads and Board Members in Groups or in personal .

No Subscription Fee will be collected from Volunteers.






Every Volunteer should maintain Discipline and should Respect over other Volunteers and Board Members.

There will be 12 Monthly Meeting + 1 Annual Meeting and Event preparation meet will be orgainsed,if a volunteer is part of the the particular event.

Each Volunteer can volunteer a event or project according to their wish, comfortability and self interest.

Best Volunteers and Project Heads will be Awarded on the basis of Co-operation, Team work, Dedication towards work and number of event attenting.
